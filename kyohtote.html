Advance Salary

(Kyo Htote - Burmese Term)

Kyo Htote is a Salary Advance Product, providing to customer who receive payroll via MoMoney Corporate Portal. This product is a financial technology solution that allows employees to access a portion of their earned wages before the traditional payday.
